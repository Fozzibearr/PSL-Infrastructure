Citation

Please cite the source of your analysis as "OG-USA release #.#.#, author's calculations." If you wish to link to OG-USA, https://github.com/open-source-economics/OG-USA is preferred. Additionally, we strongly recommend that you describe the input data used, and provide a link to the materials required to replicate your analysis or, at least, note that those materials are available upon request.
